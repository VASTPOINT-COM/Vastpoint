About

VASTPOINT, where innovation meets impact, our mission is to boost sustainable development by delivering land administration, GIS solutions, and increasing property tax collection.

Our Services

  • Data preparation, processing, analysis, and management
  • Community building and land use management
  • Mapping and land registration, boundaries delimitation, and title certification
  • Property appraisal and tax collection

Experience

Mozambique

Provision of Delimitation of Communities, Basic Land Use Planning and Land Regularization, parcels demarcation, boundaries survey and preparation and issuance of over 1.1 Million Land Titles in seven provinces.

Cabo Verde

Santiago Island Land Registry Operation clarifying the rights and boundaries of about 235,500 properties.

South Africa

Cadastre Mapping and Valuation of over 8 million properties, generating annual tax revenue of over $1 billion.

Impact

Our goal is to improve land administration in high-growth countries to promote sustainable development, ensure citizens’ provision of basic services, and improve citizens’ quality of life.
